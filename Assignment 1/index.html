About Us

ZUS Coffee: From Malaysia to the World

ZUS Coffee was founded in 2019 in Malaysia, with the mission of providing high-quality, affordable specialty coffee. Since its launch, ZUS Coffee has expanded rapidly, reaching over 586 outlets across Malaysia as of December 2024, making it the largest coffee chain in the country.

The brand name "ZUS" is not related to Zeus from Greek mythology. ZUS Coffee’s success comes from its commitment to using 100% Arabica coffee beans, expertly medium roasted to ensure a rich and well-balanced flavor in every cup. Additionally, ZUS Coffee is actively expanding into international markets, with plans to enter the Philippines, Singapore, and Brunei in the near future.

Why Choose Us

Premium Coffee Beans, Crafting a Rich and Smooth Experience

ZUS Coffee uses 100% Arabica beans, known for their smooth, rich flavor with fruity and nutty notes. Expert medium roasting enhances the natural aroma while reducing bitterness, ensuring a well-balanced, full-bodied taste. With small-batch roasting and fresh extraction, every cup delivers lasting freshness and caramel-like sweetness— a true reflection of quality and craftsmanship.
